UNC Electra

WHO ARE WE?

Electra is UNC's Women's Developmental Ultimate Frisbee team. We focus on teaching the fundamentals of the sport and work towards creating a fun and inclusive Ultimate environment.

Electra Practices 3 times a week and attends approximately 5-6 tournaments throughout the school year, which are usually located in North Carolina or Virginia.

Membership to the club team is open to all undergraduate and graduate women and gender-nonconforming individuals who are interested in playing Ultimate. No cuts will be made from this team, so all skill levels are welcome.
